# Fitlyn Help Center

Fitlyn helps Indian gyms manage members, memberships, payments, attendance,
leads, communication, reporting, and everyday front-desk work from desktop or
mobile.

## Start with these guides

- [Set up your gym](getting-started/index.md)
- [Understand roles and access](getting-started/roles.md)
- [Add and manage members](guides/members.md)
- [Create plans and memberships](guides/memberships.md)
- [Record payments and issue receipts](guides/payments.md)
- [Track attendance](guides/attendance.md)

## Main areas

| Area | What it helps you do |
|---|---|
| Dashboard | Review key gym activity and upcoming work |
| Members | Maintain member profiles and status |
| Plans and memberships | Sell, renew, extend, and track packages |
| Payments and invoices | Record collections, dues, receipts, and GST details |
| Attendance | Track manual and supported biometric check-ins |
| Leads | Follow up with prospects and convert them into members |
| Communication | Send approved WhatsApp or SMS messages |
| Reports | Review revenue, dues, renewals, and GST information |

!!! note
    Available modules and actions can depend on your Fitlyn subscription,
    configured integrations, and assigned role.
