# Privacy and safe support

Fitlyn may hold personal, attendance, communication, and financial information.
Only access or share data required for your assigned work.

## Never share

- passwords, OTPs, API keys, or provider credentials;
- complete database exports;
- payment card or banking credentials;
- unnecessary member identity or health information;
- screenshots containing unrelated customer records.

## When requesting support

Provide:

- the affected feature and environment;
- clear reproduction steps;
- approximate date and time;
- a safe member, invoice, or transaction identifier;
- a cropped screenshot with unrelated personal data hidden.

Use your organization's approved support channel. If you suspect unauthorized
access or data exposure, stop normal troubleshooting and notify the gym owner or
Fitlyn administrator immediately.
