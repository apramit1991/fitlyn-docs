# Frequently asked questions

## Why is a module missing?

Your role, subscription, or gym configuration may not allow it. Ask the gym
owner to verify your access.

## Why does monthly revenue look different?

Check the selected gym, report month, payment status, and the recorded Paid On
date. Compare the underlying payment entries before making corrections.

## How should I remove a member?

Deactivate the member so historical membership, payment, and attendance records
remain available.

## Why did a message fail?

Check the phone number, channel consent, provider configuration, template
approval, and delivery status.

## Can I edit old financial records?

Use the supported correction workflow and involve the gym owner. Avoid creating
duplicate records to compensate for an incorrect entry.

## What should I send to support?

Send the affected screen, approximate time, gym name, safe record identifier,
and steps to reproduce. Remove passwords, OTPs, payment credentials, and
unnecessary personal data.
