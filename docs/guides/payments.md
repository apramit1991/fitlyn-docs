# Payments and invoices

Fitlyn records invoices, collected amounts, dues, payment dates, payment modes,
and applicable GST details.

## Record a payment

1. Open the member invoice or Payments area.
2. Confirm the invoice and outstanding amount.
3. Enter the amount received.
4. Select the actual payment date and payment mode.
5. Save and review the updated status.
6. Preview the receipt before sharing it.

## Payment statuses

- **Due** — no collection has been recorded.
- **Partially paid** — some amount remains outstanding.
- **Paid** — the recorded invoice amount has been collected.

## Revenue dates

Monthly collection reporting uses the recorded **Paid On** date. Enter the date
on which the gym actually received the payment.

## Discounts and corrections

Apply discounts through the intended membership or invoice workflow. Avoid
creating duplicate invoices to correct an existing one. If financial history
is unclear, stop and ask the gym owner before making another entry.

## GST

Configure GST details under Settings where applicable. Review tax details on a
test receipt before using the workflow for customers. Fitlyn documentation is
not a substitute for advice from your accountant or tax professional.
