# WhatsApp and SMS communication

Communication features depend on enabled providers, approved templates, your
subscription, and the member's consent.

## Before sending

- Confirm the member's phone number.
- Confirm the member explicitly agreed to that communication channel.
- Use an approved template when required.
- Check the selected gym and recipient.
- Avoid including sensitive financial, health, or identity information.

## Broadcasts

Start with a small internal or test audience. Review the message, template,
recipient count, and channel before submitting a broadcast.

## Delivery failures

If a message fails:

1. Check whether the channel is enabled.
2. Confirm consent and the phone number.
3. Confirm the template is approved and correctly configured.
4. Review the delivery status or error shown in Fitlyn.
5. Contact support without sharing credentials or complete customer datasets.

WhatsApp and SMS providers may impose template, consent, timing, and regulatory
requirements outside Fitlyn's control.
