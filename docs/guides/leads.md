# Leads

Use Leads to track prospective members from first enquiry through conversion.

## Recommended workflow

1. Add the prospect with accurate contact details and source.
2. Record their interest and relevant plan.
3. Schedule the next follow-up.
4. Add activity notes after each interaction.
5. Update the lead status as the conversation progresses.
6. Convert the lead when they join.

When converting a lead, verify the new member profile, selected plan,
membership dates, invoice, and initial payment before completing the process.

Avoid placing sensitive health or payment information in general lead notes.
