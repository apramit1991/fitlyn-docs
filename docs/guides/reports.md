# Reports

Reports help gym owners review collections, dues, renewals, GST information,
and other operational trends.

## Before relying on a report

- Confirm the selected gym.
- Confirm the date range.
- Check that payments use the correct Paid On date.
- Confirm invoice and membership statuses.
- Compare unusual values with the underlying member or payment records.

## Common reconciliation

For a selected month:

1. Filter Payments to that month.
2. Review paid and partially paid entries.
3. Compare the collection total with Dashboard and Reports.
4. Investigate date or status differences before changing records.

Exported reports may contain personal or financial information. Store and share
them only with authorized people.
