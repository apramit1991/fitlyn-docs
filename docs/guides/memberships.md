# Plans and memberships

## Plans

Plans define what your gym sells, including the plan name, price, duration, and
availability.

Before using a plan:

- confirm its price and duration;
- use clear names that staff can distinguish;
- deactivate discontinued plans instead of reusing them for a different offer.

## Assign a membership

1. Choose the member and plan.
2. Confirm the start date.
3. Review the calculated end date and price.
4. Apply any approved discount.
5. Save and review the generated invoice or due.

## Renew or extend

- **Renew** starts a new paid membership cycle.
- **Free extension** changes the membership end date without creating another
  paid cycle.

Always confirm the resulting dates and invoice before leaving the screen.

## Personal training

When enabled, personal-training packages can track the member, trainer,
package dates, session allowance, completed sessions, fee, and payment status.
