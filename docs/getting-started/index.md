# Getting started

## 1. Sign in and complete gym setup

After signing in, complete the onboarding checklist:

1. Add your gym profile and contact details.
2. Configure applicable GST information.
3. Create at least one membership plan.
4. Add your first member.
5. Assign a membership and record a payment when applicable.

## 2. Add your team

Gym owners can invite teammates from Settings. Give each person the narrowest
role needed for their daily responsibilities.

## 3. Configure daily workflows

- Decide who adds members and records payments.
- Confirm how attendance will be captured.
- Configure WhatsApp or SMS only after the provider setup is complete.
- Review dashboard and dues information regularly.

## 4. Install the app

Fitlyn is a Progressive Web App. On supported browsers, use the browser's
**Install app** or **Add to Home screen** option for quicker access.

## Before using production data

- Confirm your gym profile and tax details.
- Test one member, membership, invoice, payment, and attendance entry.
- Confirm receipts and reports match your expected workflow.
- Use only test contact details while testing communication integrations.
