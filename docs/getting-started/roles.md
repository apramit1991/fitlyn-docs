# Roles and access

Your role controls which Fitlyn areas and actions are available.

| Role | Typical responsibilities |
|---|---|
| Gym owner | Gym setup, plans, billing, reports, team access, and oversight |
| Receptionist | Members, memberships, payments, attendance, leads, and front-desk work |
| Coach or trainer | Assigned member, training, and attendance activities |
| Fitlyn administrator | Platform support and administration |

Access may vary as Fitlyn permissions evolve. If an action is missing, contact
your gym owner before assuming it is a technical problem.

## Good access practices

- Use a separate account for each teammate.
- Do not share passwords.
- Remove access promptly when someone leaves.
- Give billing, settings, and reporting access only when required.
- Review team access periodically.
